## The Journey

I started handbuilding and throwing pottery in high school art class. When I came back for sophomore year I told the art teacher that I wasn't doing her regular curriculum, just ceramics. She agreed, probably because I spent all my free periods and after school time in the studio the previous year.. and I was hooked.

After nearly 20 years of traditional pottery, I discovered 3D printed ceramics and found a new passion. Check out [Pottery Math](/projects/pottery_math), and [LF3DP](/projects/lf3dp) for some of my explorations in digital ceramics fabrication!
